Biography:

I am a final year M.S. candidate at University of Houston-Clear Lake (UHCL). I received my B.Tech. from Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT) in 2016. During my bachelors, I worked on some extracurricular projects. Demos for few of them are available on my YouTube channel.
