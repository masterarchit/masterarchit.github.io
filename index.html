Biography:

I am a final year M.S. candidate at University of Houston, Clear Lake (UHCL). I received my B.Tech. from Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT) in 2016. During freshman and sophomore, not only to hone skills but to explore more in the corresponding field, my friends and I worked on some cool projects. Demos for few of them are available on my YouTube channel. I also worked as a research intern at DA-IICT under the guidance of Dr. Amit Bhatt. Moreover, I worked as a System Developer Intern at Inweon, mentored by Akshat Mittal and Dr. Milind Padalkar.

Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant with my advisor Dr. Xiaokun Yang and Teaching Assistant with Dr. Hakduran Koc and Professor Hoang Minh Nguyen at UHCL.

Research Interests:

Academic Projects:

Low-Cost Security System Using Off the Shelf Components January 2016 - April 2016
  • As my final year B.Tech project, I developed a security system capturing an image of an intruder and informing the designated person through e-mail using Simple Mail Transfer Protocol (SMTP). The cost of the developed system was almost a half of the price compared to the similar products available in the commercial market.
System Developer Intern, Inweon May 2015 - October 2015
  • I developed code to find the length of rice particle from a given image. Later, Raspberry pi micro-controller was set up to perform the code remotely.
Research Intern, DA-IICT May 2015 - July 2015
  • Under the guidance of a professor, gained knowledge of Cadence's Encounter, register-transfer level (RTL) and tickle files. I also learned about the trade-off between power, area, and performance for a couple of micro-controllers.
Surveillance Bot January 2015 - April 2015
  • A live video stream from a camera mounted on a manually controlled surveillance robot using Beagle Bone Black.
5-Stage Pipeline processor July 2014 - November 2014
  • A 5 stage pipeline processor was developed using Verilog, which could perform basic 13 tasks like addition, multiplication, logical shift left/right, no operation (NOP) etc.
